# MVP Studio Outreach System

## Goal
Book discovery calls with businesses that can pay for custom software ($15k-$100k+ projects).

## Target Cities
1. **Chicago** - Alex's home base, existing network
2. **New York** - Largest market, enterprise density
3. **Los Angeles** - Tech-adjacent, entertainment, startups

## Target Company Profile
- **Revenue:** $5M-$500M (big enough to pay, small enough to need external dev)
- **Employee count:** 50-2000
- **Industries:** 
  - Professional services (law, accounting, consulting)
  - Healthcare (clinics, medical groups, health tech)
  - Real estate (brokerages, property management)
  - Manufacturing (with modernization needs)
  - Financial services (wealth management, insurance)
  - Logistics & distribution
  - Hospitality & restaurants (multi-location)
  - Media & entertainment (LA focus)

## Target Contacts
- CEO / Founder (small companies)
- CTO / VP Engineering (if they exist but need help)
- COO / VP Operations (internal tools)
- Head of Product / Digital (modernization)

## Research Process (per company)
1. Company website - what they do, pain points visible
2. LinkedIn - company size, key people, recent posts
3. News - recent funding, growth, challenges
4. Tech stack (if visible) - BuiltWith, job postings
5. Reviews/complaints - where software could help

## Email Structure
- **Subject:** Specific to their pain point
- **Opening:** Reference something specific about them
- **Problem:** Pain point they likely have
- **Solution:** How custom software solves it (1-2 sentences)
- **Proof:** Brief relevant experience
- **CTA:** 15-min call, link to Calendly

## Cadence
- 5 new companies researched + emailed per hour
- Mix cities: 2 Chicago, 2 NYC, 1 LA per batch
- Track opens/replies if possible
- Follow up after 3 days if no response
